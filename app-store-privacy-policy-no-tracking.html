Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile applications published on both the Apple App Store and Google Play. It explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information.

Our apps may collect limited technical data automatically for functionality and analytics, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and crash logs

We do not access or use your Advertising ID (IDFA or AAID).

2. Advertising

Our apps use third-party advertising services:

  • Google AdMob
  • Unity Ads

These services are used to show non-personalized ads.

We have disabled personalized ad targeting and user tracking.

No data is linked across apps or websites for advertising or measurement purposes.

For more information on their privacy practices, you may review:

  • Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Unity Ads: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

3. Data Use

Collected data is used solely to:

  • Display non-personalized ads
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Diagnose and fix crashes

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Ad networks may process limited, non-identifiable information as described above for ad delivery.

5. Tracking

We do not track users across apps or websites.

We do not request or use tracking permissions (App Tracking Transparency or similar).

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are suitable for general audiences and do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

7. Security

We use standard security practices to protect all collected data.
