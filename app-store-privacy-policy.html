App Store Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use our iOS applications available on the App Store.

Information We Collect

Identifiers

  • Device Identifier (IDFV)
  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA) (only if you grant tracking permission via App Tracking Transparency)

Usage and Diagnostic Data

  • App version, session duration, feature usage statistics, performance metrics, and crash reports

Purchase Information

  • In-app purchase receipts, product IDs, and transaction status (used to validate purchases through StoreKit)

Log Data

  • Timestamps and technical details needed to diagnose errors and analyze performance

How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality – Provide core features, verify purchases, prevent fraud, and improve stability
  • Analytics – Understand app performance and user interaction to guide updates
  • Advertising and Attribution – If you grant permission, use device identifiers for personalized ads and measurement across other apps and websites
  • Support and Security – Respond to inquiries and protect against abuse

Tracking and Your Choices

We request App Tracking Transparency permission before accessing the IDFA. Tracking means linking data from this app with data from other companies' apps or websites for advertising or measurement purposes.

If you choose "Ask App Not to Track," we will not access the IDFA and will not use any other method to track you. You can change this choice anytime in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third parties to deliver ads, analyze usage, and handle transactions.

Service Purpose Data Shared

Each third party operates under its own privacy policy.

Data Security and Retention

  • Data is encrypted during transmission.
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described here and then securely deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your data. Submit requests by email below.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn we have done so, we will delete that data.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We apply appropriate safeguards as required by law.
